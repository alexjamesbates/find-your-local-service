Hire a commode

You can hire a commode or toilet aids from the British Red Cross for short-term use.

We can provide:

  • commode
  • toilet seat
  • toilet frame

You can also buy these from us.

Find your local commode service

How it works

You can find your local service and contact them directly.

You can then:

  • visit your local service to collect your commode
  • arrange home delivery (for an extra fee)

Bring some photo identification, eg passport or driving licence, and proof of your address.

How much it costs

We charge a small amount for the commode pot which can’t be reused. We’ll ask for a donation for the other equipment.

Returning your commode

Contact your local service to arrange to return your commode

It’s important to return your commode or toilet aids on the date agreed. You don’t need to return the commode pot.
