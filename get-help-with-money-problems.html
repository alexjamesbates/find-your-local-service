Get help with money problems

If you’re struggling with money problems the British Red Cross can help you through a period of financial difficulty.

How we can help

You can get advice online from Turn2Us, including:

  • what benefits you may be entitled to
  • charitable grants you can apply for
  • how to reduce fuel bills
  • local services that could support you
Get advice online




Or call the free helpline: 0808 802 2000
Monday to Friday, 9am to 8pm
